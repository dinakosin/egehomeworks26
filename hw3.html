Kathy: Sorry, can you spell your name, please?

Fatma: Yes, it's F-a-t-m-a.

Kathy: Welcome, Fatma! Where are you from?

Sanjit: No, I'm not. I'm French, but my parents are from India.

04 Use the prompts & be 🧩

Use contractions where possible.

1. she / from / Beijing. (Пример: She's from Beijing.)
2. he / a businessman.
3. it / not / in London.
4. where / Sophie / from?
5. he / a teacher?
6. what / Jack's job?
7. she / not / an office worker. She / a shop assistant.
8. the Colosseum / not / in Milan. It / in Rome.
